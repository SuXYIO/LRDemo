i/init [args]
	Init values for neuron. 
	Options: 
		-n [neuronname]
			Define which neuron to init, a is default. 
			neuronname:
				a: All, f & g
				f: main neuron. 
				g: training neruon. 
		-m [methodname]
			Define which init method to use, 1 is default. 
			methodname: 
				0: All to 0.0. 
				1: Normal distro (mean = 0.0, stddev = 1.0, box-muller approach).
